Welcome to the Recolon Documentation¶

Recolon is a modern, experimental programming language designed to blend simplicity with powerful capabilities. Whether you’re a beginner looking to learn programming or an experienced developer seeking a new tool for your projects, Recolon offers an intuitive syntax and a strong foundation to help you achieve your goals.

This documentation will guide you through everything from setting up your environment to mastering advanced features. Explore the core concepts, standard modules, and unique functionalities that make Recolon stand out.

Get started by setting up Recolon on your machine, then dive into the basics and beyond. Welcome to the Recolon community—let’s start coding!
